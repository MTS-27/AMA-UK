About The Association of Muslim Apprentices

The Association of Muslim Apprentices (AMA) is a grassroots, youth-led organisation dedicated to supporting, representing, and empowering Muslim apprentices across the UK. We believe that apprenticeships are not only powerful tools for career development, but also essential for building inclusive and economically empowered communities.

Founded in response to the lack of visibility and access for Muslims in the apprenticeship world, AMA exists to challenge stereotypes, open up opportunities, and build a supportive network for young Muslims navigating vocational pathways.

Our mission is simple: increase awareness, provide guidance, and ensure Muslim voices are heard in every sector of the apprenticeship ecosystem. We work closely with employers, community leaders, and government bodies to drive structural change and open new doors for the next generation.

Our Values

  • Representation: Amplifying the voices of Muslim apprentices nationwide.
  • Equity: Advocating for fair access and opportunities for all backgrounds.
  • Faith and Identity: Providing space for Muslims to thrive without compromise.

What We Offer

  • School outreach and apprenticeship talks in mosques and community hubs
  • 1-to-1 mentorship and application support for Muslim students
  • Community events and digital resources designed by Muslim apprentices
